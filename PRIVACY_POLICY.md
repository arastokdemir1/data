# CarLog Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

CarLog is a trip cost assistant for iOS. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

## Data Stored On Device

CarLog may store vehicles, saved locations, odometer entries, fuel or charging logs, and trip configuration on your device using Apple's local storage frameworks. This data is used to calculate trip costs and show your garage history.

## Network Data

CarLog may fetch public pricing data such as fuel prices, toll tariff files, vehicle catalog data, Apple MapKit route results, and StoreKit product information. These requests are used to provide pricing estimates, route alternatives, and subscription status.

CarLog does not sell personal data.

## Location

When you choose to calculate a route, CarLog may request your location and send route start and destination data to Apple MapKit. Location access is used only for route planning and trip cost estimation.

## Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. CarLog receives entitlement status from Apple but does not process payment card details.

## Third-Party Services

CarLog may use:

- Apple MapKit for maps and route calculations.
- Apple StoreKit for purchases and subscriptions.
- Public pricing data endpoints for fuel, toll, and vehicle catalog updates.
